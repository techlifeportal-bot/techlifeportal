About TechLifePortal

TechLifePortal is a fully AI-powered lifestyle and career assistant created for IT employees living and working in Bangalore. The platform helps tech professionals quickly find cafés, gyms, PGs, rentals, and fresh job opportunities around major tech hubs like Electronic City, Whitefield, Bellandur, Manyata Tech Park, and Ecospace.

Our Mission

To simplify the daily life of every IT employee by providing fast, automated, and accurate information about everything they need — from finding a good place to stay, to discovering cafés for remote work, to identifying weekend getaways, to exploring new job opportunities.

How It Works

  • ✔ AI automatically fetches cafés, PGs, and rentals based on the area you select.
  • ✔ Gyms, rentals, and cafés update live using free map data + curated results.
  • ✔ Job listings for Bangalore refresh automatically (freshers + experienced roles).
  • ✔ Experiences posted by users stay saved on their device and will soon be stored globally.
