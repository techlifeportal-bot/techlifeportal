ℹ️About TechLifePortal

TechLifePortal is an AI-guided lifestyle and career companion created especially for IT employees across Bangalore’s tech corridors.

1. Who is behind this?

This project is built and led by KB, who is using AI as a full-time partner to develop a real product without a traditional coding background.

2. What does the site do?

  • Helps you discover cafés around major hubs like Electronic City, Whitefield, Bellandur, Manyata and Ecospace.
  • Lists nearby gyms and basic fitness options per area.
  • Shows example PGs, hostels, co-living spaces and flats around tech parks.
  • Shows example IT companies around each tech hub.
  • Provides a curated list of IT job openings for freshers and junior engineers in Bangalore.
  • Lets IT folks share their own TechLife stories directly on the site.

3. How is it built?

Every part of TechLifePortal — layout, content, automation ideas — is built step-by-step with AI guidance, while KB executes the changes live and learns the process.

4. What’s next?

  • More accurate real-time data for PGs, rentals and gyms.
  • Support for more Indian cities and tech parks.
  • Optional user accounts, saved favourites and smarter job recommendations.
